# Edu-Digital

Edu-Digital is an independent digital literacy initiative based in Portugal.

The project explores digital culture, technology and the way digital systems shape society, education and everyday life.

## Focus

Edu-Digital explores topics such as:

- digital literacy
- digital culture
- the social impact of technology
- artificial intelligence and society
- digital citizenship

## What the project creates

- articles and essays
- workshops and sessions
- digital experiments
- educational resources

## About

Edu-Digital was created by Joana Contente, working at the intersection of technology, education and human experience.
